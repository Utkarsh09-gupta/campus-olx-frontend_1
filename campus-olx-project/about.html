Our Mission

Campus OLX is a student-driven initiative at **Lucknow University** to create a sustainable and affordable ecosystem for academic resources. We believe that knowledge should be shared, and resources should be reused.

Safe & Secure

Exclusively for verified LU students, ensuring a trustworthy community for trading.

Eco-Friendly

By reusing books and equipment, we reduce waste and promote sustainability on campus.

Affordable

Get high-quality academic materials at a fraction of the market price.

Community

Built by students, for students. Helping each other succeed academically.
